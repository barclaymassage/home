Frequently Asked Questions & Policies

What is included in the rate?

Every session is completely tailored to your clinical assessment. Deep tissue work, trigger point therapy, and customized stretching are fully integrated into your time block at no additional charge.

What is your cancellation policy?

Your appointment time is reserved exclusively for you. Cancellations or changes made with less than 24 hours' notice are subject to a fee up to the full value of the scheduled service.

Do you accept HSA/FSA cards?

Yes. If your insurance plan covers massage therapy for medical necessity or injury rehabilitation, you can use your Health Savings Account (HSA) or Flexible Spending Account (FSA) card at checkout. A detailed receipt with appropriate standard billing codes can be provided upon request.
